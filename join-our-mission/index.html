SmartChessMath transforms chess into a practical educational tool for teaching mathematics, developing thinking skills, and supporting primary schools with engaging digital learning experiences.

Learning Becomes an Adventure

Children learn better when they feel excited, challenged, and proud of their progress.

Our programs combine missions, stories, problem solving, certificates, and school competitions so that every child feels that learning is active, joyful, and meaningful.

Think Like a Computer

SmartChessMath supports computational thinking without turning children into passive users of technology.

Students learn to plan steps, test ideas, compare choices, correct mistakes, and improve their strategy — the same thinking habits needed in the age of artificial intelligence.

AI for Education

Smart analytical tools can help teachers and schools understand student progress, identify learning patterns, and support better educational decisions.

  • Monitor class progress and student development.
  • Support teachers with meaningful learning insights.
  • Help schools improve outcomes through data-informed planning.
  • Encourage every learner with a clearer path forward.

Technology supports teachers. It never replaces them.
