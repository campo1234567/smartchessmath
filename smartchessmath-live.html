Why it matters

A modern school platform, not just a game.

SmartChessMath Live V1.0 helps teachers turn mathematics into an active, visual, and measurable classroom experience.

🎓

Easy for teachers

Open a classroom, select an activity, explain live, and guide students step by step.

🧠

Thinking skills

Students practice logic, planning, attention, and exact calculation through chess movement.

🏆

Competition ready

Official challenges, stars, rankings, medals, reports, and certificates support school events.

📊

Clear progress

Teachers and schools can observe student performance, speed, accuracy, and participation.

What the teacher can do

  • Create a live classroom for a school group.
  • Explain questions before the challenge starts.
  • Let a selected student solve while everyone watches.
  • Start official rounds and monitor participation.
  • Print reports and certificates after the challenge.

What students experience

  • Join from a teacher link or classroom code.
  • Follow the teacher explanation in real time.
  • Solve chess-math challenges on their own device.
  • Compete safely with classmates.
  • Build confidence through visible progress.
